SCORING

We rank movies One out of five stars. On this page A rating of 1 star means the movie was not enjoyable or well-made, while 5 stars indicates a fantastic film. The reviewers consider various factors like acting, plot, cinematography, and overall entertainment value to determine the rating.

  1. One Stars means that the movie was really bad.

  2. Two stars means the movie wasn't the best and can be improved alot.

  3. Three stars means the movie was decent and wasn't the best but wasn't the worst.

  4. Four stars means that the movie was pretty good and had a good plot.

  5. Five stars means that the movie was outstanding and one of the best i've ever seen.
